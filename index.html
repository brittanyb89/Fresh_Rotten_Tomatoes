Game Rules

You will be given 2 reviews from diffrent movies. 1 will be from a genre you selected and the other will be from a random genre. Select the movie review from the genre you selected to gain points. selecting the wrong review will NOT subtract points. To start the game, select a genre and click start. You will answer 10 questions and the game will end.
